About Me

The story behind
every frame.

For over three years I've chased light across cities, coastlines and quiet backstreets, building a practice that treats every shoot as its own short film. My work sits at the intersection of documentary honesty and cinematic polish — never staged, always intentional.

I've partnered with couples, founders, and brands who wanted their story told with craft, not just captured with a camera. Every frame goes through the same obsessive process: brief, shoot, edit, deliver — refined until it feels inevitable.

When I'm not behind the lens, I'm scouting new locations, studying color grading, or tuning a drone flight path for the next golden hour.
